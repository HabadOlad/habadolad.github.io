Bio

My earliest code experience was building mini-sites for my Neopets in the late '90s, an initial spark that lay dormant while I pursued a deep passion for the environment and sciences. I earned an MSc in Environmental Technology from Imperial College London and spent my early career contributing to the UK's largest infrastructure developments, such as High Speed 2 (HS2) and the Heathrow Terminal Expansion. This work honed my skills in project management, stakeholder relationship building, and operating in complex, high-pressure environments. My passion for code was reignited in data analytics, where I became proficient in using Python (NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn) and VB Basic to transform raw CSV data into actionable insights.
